Bear Snake Game

A fun twist on the classic Snake game

About This Game

Bear Snake is a variation of the classic Snake game with a cute bear theme. Control the bear, collect food, and grow as long as possible without hitting walls or yourself!

Score: 0
Press Start to play!

How to Play

  • Controls: Use arrow keys or WASD keys, or the on-screen buttons to control the bear's movement
  • Red Food: Collect red food to grow longer and earn 10 points
  • Golden Food: Special golden food appears occasionally for 50 bonus points
  • Dangers: Avoid hitting the walls or your own body
